FAQ

COMMON QUESTIONS

What app do you use for online coaching? +
Josh uses a dedicated coaching app that houses your full program, exercise videos, progress tracking, and direct messaging. You'll get full onboarding once you're accepted.
I travel a lot — can you work around my schedule? +
Absolutely. Programming is built around your life — not the other way around. Travel, busy weeks, and schedule changes are all accounted for. This is one of the biggest advantages of working with Josh.
Is nutrition coaching really included? +
Yes — always. Josh believes you can't separate training from nutrition. Every program includes real-food guidance, grocery strategies, and habit-based eating principles from day one. No extra charge.
What fitness level do I need to be at? +
All levels are welcome. Whether you're just getting started or you're an experienced athlete looking to break through a plateau, Josh meets you exactly where you are and builds from there.
Where are in-person sessions located? +
In-person sessions are based in the Denver, Colorado area. Reach out after applying and Josh will share the specific location details.
